|---|---|

| **Actual** | Sum of Itinerary `Expense.amount` by currency |
| **Difference** | `Actual âˆ’ Planned` by currency â€” **new in v3.4.0 at Itinerary level** |

## Added

### `trip export-md` Itinerary section

When an itinerary has **both** Estimates and Expenses, a summary appears after the estimate table and expense lines:

```text
Planned total:
  - JPY 14,000
Actual total:
  - JPY 13,750
Difference:
  - JPY -250
```

- Difference is calculated as **`Actual âˆ’ Planned`**
- Shown **only** when the itinerary has both Estimates and Expenses
- Per-currency calculation with **no FX conversion**
- Keys = union of planned and actual currencies; missing side treated as **0**

Trip-level Overview Planned / Actual / Difference (**v3.3.0**) is unchanged.
